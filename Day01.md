# What is DevOps?

## Overview

**DevOps** is a culture and set of practices that bring **software development (Dev)** and **IT operations (Ops)** together to deliver software **faster**, **more reliably**, and with **higher quality**.

## Why DevOps?

Traditional software delivery often suffers from:

* Slow releases
* Manual processes
* Poor communication between teams
* Late detection of bugs

DevOps addresses these challenges by improving the entire software delivery lifecycle.
